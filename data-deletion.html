Data Deletion Instructions

Last updated: March 3, 2025

At Poshan, we respect your privacy and give you control over your data. This page explains how you can request deletion of your personal data from our systems.

Your Data Deletion Options

Option 1: Delete Your Account Within the App

The simplest way to delete your data is through the app:

  1. Open the Poshan app
  2. Go to Profile â†’ Settings
  3. Scroll down and tap "Delete My Account"
  4. Follow the confirmation steps

This will permanently delete all your personal information, order history, and preferences from our systems.

Option 2: Email Request

If you prefer, you can email us to request data deletion:

  1. Send an email to privacy@example.com
  2. Use the subject line "Data Deletion Request"
  3. Include the email address associated with your account
  4. We will process your request within 30 days and send a confirmation email

What Data We Delete

When you request data deletion, we remove:

  • Your account information (name, email, phone number)
  • Your order history
  • Your saved preferences and settings
  • Any payment information stored in our systems
  • Your activity and usage data

Data Retention

We may retain certain information as required by law or for legitimate business purposes for a limited period after deletion. For example, we may keep some data for financial reporting obligations or to prevent fraud.

Third-Party Data

If you used social login (like Facebook, Google, or Apple) to create your account, we will disconnect your account from these services. However, you may need to visit these platforms separately to manage data you shared with them.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about data deletion, please contact us at privacy@example.com.
